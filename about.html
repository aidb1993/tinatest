About
Tina Tombs

Born in Montreal, Quebec, in 1962, and raised in Bedford, NH. Married to Larry Fischer. Two daughters: Sarah and Heidi. Graduate of Arizona State University with a BS in Physical Education, Minor in Anatomy and Physiology. Founder and President of Tina Tombs Golf, LLC @ The Arizona Biltmore Golf Club in Phoenix Arizona - 2002 to present.

After receiving numerous full athletic scholar- ships for both swimming and golf, Tina accepted the offer from Arizona State University and graduated with a B.S. degree in Physical Education and a minor in Anatomy and Physiology. Tina’s time at ASU saw the rise of her flourishing golf career, and in the fall of 2006 she was inducted into ASU’s Athletic Hall of Fame. Amongst her many accomplishments, in 1984, she was ranked among Golf Digest’s top 10 amateur golfers in the USA.

In 1987, she qualified for the LPGA Tour and began a 14 year career with the LPGA Touring division. The highlight of her LPGA career was winning the 1990 Jamie Farr Toledo Classic. As the founder and President of Tina Tombs Golf at the Arizona Biltmore Golf Club, she holds the role of Director of Instruction, as well as carries out the responsibility to oversee and organize the overall education and teaching programs for the Tina Tombs Golf Learning Center. These highly sought out programs are designed to benefit the students and provide a well-rounded and individually structured program that follows the LPGA Teaching and Education Programs. Tina’s work does not stop at the golf course. She also engages with the community through her involvement in the following charities: Wings of Gold as Arizona State University, Barrow’s Neu- rological Institute, Boys and Girls Club and vari- ous Breast Cancer organizations nationally. In addition to these roles, as the LPGA Teacher of the Year it is her honor and responsibility to carry out the mission of the LPGA.

In 2004 Tina decided to continue her golfing career by establishing a premier teaching academy at the Arizona Biltmore Golf Club. Tina uses state of the art technology in her teaching academy. This includes JC Video swing analysis, Two Machine Vision Cameras, Flightscope Launch Monitor and Smart2Move foot pressure plates. These tools are all included in her individual golf lessons, clinics and corporate functions. She is a certified AimPoint Express green reading instructor, as well as a certified Bioswing Dynamics instructor. Tina Tombs teaches the "game within the game".

Our teaching session: during your private, group, corporate or clinic session we will incorporate a balanced curriculum with an individual emphasis on bio mechanics and a personal attention to each athlete. Our philosophy is based on proper golf club fitting, peak mental performance training, course management, and health fitness. Our ability to customize instruction and coaching techniques to individual goals, different styles of learning, and personal abilities ensures a tailored approach to help individuals elevate their skills, and add enjoyment to the great game of golf.
