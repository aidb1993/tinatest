Tina Tombs named a Golf Tips TOP 25 Instructor

Tina Tombs Named A Golf Tips Top 25 Instructor

Braintree, Mass. — Golf Tips magazine has named Arizona-based LPGA teaching professionalTina Tombs one of the Top 25 Instructors in America for 2019-2020. Tina owns and operates the “Tina Tombs Golf” Learning Center at the Arizona Biltmore Golf Club in Phoenix, Arizona. She has been at there for over fifteen years. A sampling of the many accolades bestowed on Tina includes: two time, and current LPGA National Teacher of the year(2018, 2014). She was voted “Best in State Instructor” by Golf Digest Magazine 2017-2018, LPGA Top 50 Best Instructors, US Kids Top 50 Instructors, and honored with the Edgar R. Updegraff Award from the Arizona Golf Association, which represents their highest award of recognition for exemplifying the spirit of the game. Tina enjoyed both a successful amateur and professional career, winning multiple times, as well as winning the 1990 Jamie Farr Toledo Championship, a flagship tournament on the professional LPGA Tour. Tina joins two dozen of the nation’s most respected and lauded teachers to form what editor Vic Williams calls “the ultimate teaching team” who will bring their extensive instruction and coaching knowledge to the bi-monthly, nationally distributed print magazine, and to a fast-growing online readership at www.golftipsmag.com. Working with the Golf Tips editorial and digital staffs, Top 25 Instructors will provide well-illustrated, detailed tips and lessons, as well as video content, for all aspects of the game — from fundamentals to course management, equipment fitting to the mental game.

“After months of researching and vetting an incredibly rich field of candidates for our new Top 25, we believe we’ve come up with an inaugural collection of the best and brightest minds in the American golf teaching business,” Williams says. “Since 1989 Golf Tips has built a strongreputation as the go-to source for straightforward, accessible and indispensable instruction for golfers of all ages and abilities. The new Top 25 will only deepen that reputation while helping our readers enjoy the game more than ever. We are proud to include Tina in the Golf Tips Top 25 family.” Over the month of November 2018, one new Top 25 Instructor will be introduced at www.golftipsmag.com each weekday, as well as on Twitter @golftipsmag and at Golf Tips Magazine on Facebook. The January-February 2019 of the Golf Tips print edition will include a special Top 25 section. “This is an exciting time for Golf Tips, for the thousands of loyal readers who demand the best golf instruction we can provide, and for the game of golf in general,” Williams says. “Tina and her Top 25 colleagues are committed to making Golf Tips the best it can be.” Golf Tips is part of the Massachusetts-based Madavor family of respected media properties covering four content divisions — Enthusiast, Creative, Aviation and Wellness. For more information visit www.madavor.com.
