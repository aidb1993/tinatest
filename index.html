Tina Tombs

Master Instructor

As the founder and President of Tina Tombs Golf at the Arizona Biltmore Golf Club, she holds the role of Director of Instruction, as well as carries out the responsibility to oversee and organize the overall education and teaching programs for the Tina Tombs Golf Learning Center.

Tina is recognized as one of GOLF Magazine's TOP 100 Instructors, The LPGA T&CP Two-Time National Teacher of The Year, LPGA Top 50 Teachers, Golf Digest Best in State and Golf Tips Magazine Top 25 Instructors.
